About Me

Hi, I'm Piyaporn Lahr!
My first experience that I know about programming and coding was in high school. I remembered choosing the computer club just because of my interest in playing computer games that time. What I was taught in the class was a basic programming. Back then I enjoyed it and thought how cool it would be to know how to code or create a programme. However as a young asian girl, it was difficult to choose to do something that is completely unknown to the family, so I ended up choosing and becoming a registered nurse in Thailand since 2012 then moved to London in 2019.

Having worked in health care for ten years, I am reliable, experienced in working with others to achieve a certain objective on time and with excellence. I am a fast learner with a desire to learn. With my passion in IT, I decided to give myself the opportunity to step into it by attending AWS Re/start programme and now I undertake a 12-week intensive coding, programming and software development training at _nology.
